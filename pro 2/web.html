About Our Restaurant

At Crust Pizza, we believe in crafting the perfect pizza experience with the freshest ingredients and bold, unique flavors. Whether you’re a fan of classic Margherita, crave a spicy Jerk Chicken pizza, or want to try something new with our signature Old Smokey, we have something for every pizza lover. Our mission is to bring people together with the love of good food, great conversations, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. We handcraft each pizza with care, using traditional techniques combined with a modern twist to ensure every bite is bursting with flavor. Dine with us for a cozy meal, or enjoy the convenience of our online ordering for takeout and delivery. At Crust Pizza, you're always just one bite away from pizza heaven!

Chef Vikas Khanna

Chef Vikas Khanna is a celebrated culinary icon renowned for his innovative approach to Indian cuisine. With multiple Michelin stars to his name, he has established himself as one of the leading chefs in the world. Chef Khanna's culinary journey began in India, where he honed his skills before moving to the United States, where he opened the acclaimed restaurant Junoon in New York City. He is also a prolific author, having published several cookbooks that showcase his passion for Indian flavors and ingredients. Beyond his culinary pursuits, Chef Khanna is recognized for his philanthropic efforts, particularly in advocating for hunger relief and education initiatives in India. His achievements include numerous prestigious awards, such as the James Beard Award and the "Global Citizen Award" from the United Nations, highlighting his commitment to culinary excellence and social responsibility.
